# FAQ corpus (minimal RAG demo)

Chunks are delimited by `##` headings below. Each section is one retrieval unit for `scripts/rag_faq_smoke.py`.

## Returns and refunds

We process refunds within 14 business days of approval. You need the order number and the email used at checkout. Digital goods may have different return windows shown at purchase.

## Shipping delays

If your tracking shows "exception," contact the carrier first with the tracking ID. We cannot reroute a parcel that has left our warehouse, but we can file a support ticket after 5 business days without movement.

## Account security

Enable two-factor authentication in Settings > Security. If you see a login you did not make, change your password immediately and review active sessions. We will never ask for your full card number in chat.

## Billing

Invoices are emailed monthly. Failed payments retry automatically twice; after that the account is paused until you update the payment method in the billing portal.
